Classic Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of all -purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet of dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mopzzarella cheese
  • fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Mix yeast and warm water, let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour and knead into a dought, let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out the dough and spred tomato sauce on top
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and bake at 200°C (400°F) for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
